## P1: Narrator Never Concludes for Reader

### Xuanhuan (玄幻)

Power-up moments are the highest-risk area. AI tends to write "He finally understood the true meaning of the dao" — delete and let the breakthrough action speak.

**zh**: ✗ "他终于领悟了大道的真谛" → ✓ 只写突破时的身体变化和周围环境反应
**en**: ✗ "He finally grasped the essence of the dao" → ✓ Write the physical transformation and environmental reaction

Acceptable exception: An elder character narrating a lesson may conclude — this is characterization, not narrator preaching.

### Xianxia (仙侠)

Cultivation breakthroughs have the same risk as xuanhuan. Additionally, watch for moral conclusions: "He understood that the righteous path was the only way."

**zh**: ✗ "他明白了正道才是唯一的路" → ✓ 写他的选择和行动
**en**: ✗ "He realized righteousness was the only true path" → ✓ Write his choice and action

### LitRPG

System notifications naturally "conclude" — this is part of the genre convention and acceptable. But narrator conclusions outside system text are still banned.

**zh**: System message "恭喜突破" is fine; narrator "他知道这次突破改变了一切" is not.
**en**: System notification "[Level Up!]" is fine; narrator "He knew this level-up changed everything" is not.

### Modern (现代)

Emotional realizations are the highest-risk area. "She finally understood what love really meant" — delete. Let the scene speak.

**zh**: ✗ "她终于明白了爱的真谛" → ✓ 写她的行为变化
**en**: ✗ "She finally understood what love meant" → ✓ Write her changed behavior

## P2: No Analytical/Report Language in Prose

### Xuanhuan

Combat analysis is the main trap. Characters assessing opponent strength should think like a fighter, not a military analyst.

**zh**: ✗ "对方的核心优势在于速度" → ✓ "这家伙快得邪乎"
**en**: ✗ "His opponent's core advantage was speed" → ✓ "The bastard was fast — too fast"

### Xianxia

Sect politics scenes often trigger analytical language. Characters scheming should think in colloquial terms.

**zh**: ✗ "当前的战略格局对他不利" → ✓ "他现在左右不是人"
**en**: ✗ "The current strategic landscape was unfavorable" → ✓ "He was stuck between two sides, and both wanted him dead"

### LitRPG

System analysis screens use analytical language by convention — this is acceptable within system UI. Character thoughts about the system should still be colloquial.

**zh**: System: "伤害计算: 基础攻击力 × 暴击倍率" (acceptable). Character: ✗ "核心策略是提升暴击" → ✓ "暴击拉满，莽就完了"
**en**: System: "Damage calc: Base ATK × Crit multiplier" (acceptable). Character: ✗ "The optimal strategy is crit-stacking" → ✓ "Stack crit. Hit hard. Simple."

### Modern

Business, legal, and academic settings may have characters who genuinely think analytically — but it should read as personality, not as narrator analysis.

**zh**: ✗ Narrator: "信息不对称是核心风险" → ✓ Character thought: "对方知道的比我多，这才是麻烦"
**en**: ✗ Narrator: "Information asymmetry was the core risk" → ✓ Character thought: "They know more than I do. That's the problem."

## P3: AI-Tell Words Frequency Control

### Xuanhuan

Genre-specific high-risk words (zh): 仿佛, 宛如, 竟然, 猛然 — these appear at extreme frequency in xuanhuan AI output. Replace with specific cultivation-world sensory details.

**zh**: ✗ "他仿佛感到一股力量" → ✓ "丹田里有东西在转"
**en**: ✗ "He seemed to feel a surge of power" → ✓ "Something turned in his dantian"

### Xianxia

Same as xuanhuan, with additional risk from poetic language: 宛若, 恍如 tend to cluster in xianxia AI output.

### LitRPG

Lower risk — the genre's direct style naturally avoids flowery words. Watch for: "suddenly" / "忽然" in combat descriptions.

### Modern

High-risk words (en): delve, tapestry, nuanced, landscape (metaphorical). These are AI-tell signatures regardless of genre.

**en**: ✗ "The nuanced landscape of their relationship" → ✓ "Things between them were complicated — in the way that only comes from years of small betrayals"

## P4: No Repetitive Image Cycling

### Xuanhuan

Fire, ice, and lightning imagery cycle constantly in cultivation battle scenes. After using a fire image twice, switch to a completely different sensory channel (sound, smell, spatial awareness).

**zh**: First: "火焰吞噬了他的经脉" → Second: "灼烧感扩散到四肢" → Third: ✗ "火焰再次涌来" → ✓ "他听见自己骨头在响"

### Xianxia

Sword qi / spiritual pressure imagery cycles. Same rule: third occurrence must shift to new sensory information.

### LitRPG

Damage descriptions cycle ("pain shot through him"). Switch to system feedback, spatial awareness, or tactical observation.

### Modern

Emotional metaphors cycle (heart sinking, stomach turning). After two visceral metaphors, switch to environmental detail or action.

**en**: First: "Her stomach dropped." → Second: "That familiar nausea." → Third: ✗ "Her gut twisted." → ✓ "She picked up the glass and set it down without drinking."

## P5: Planning Terms Never in Chapter Text

### All Genres

This principle has no genre adaptation — planning terms are system-internal regardless of genre. The banned terms list applies universally:

- 当前处境 / current situation
- 核心动机 / core motivation
- 信息边界 / information boundary
- 性格过滤 / personality filter
- 行为选择 / behavioral choice

These are PRE_WRITE_CHECK tools. If they appear in chapter text, it is always a leak.

## P6: Minimize "Not X; Y" Construction

### Xuanhuan

Cultivation revelations are high-risk: "This was not strength — it was something beyond strength."

**zh**: ✗ "这不是力量，是超越力量的东西" → ✓ Name the specific cultivation concept directly
**en**: ✗ "This wasn't power — it was something beyond power" → ✓ Name the specific ability

### Xianxia

Philosophical moments: "It wasn't about the sword — it was about the heart."

**zh**: ✗ "不是剑的问题，是心的问题" → ✓ Describe the insight through action, not negation-correction

### LitRPG

Lower risk — the genre's direct style naturally avoids this pattern. Watch for: "It wasn't a bug — it was a feature" type constructions.

### Modern

Relationship revelations are highest risk: "It wasn't love. It was something else entirely."

**en**: ✗ "It wasn't love. It was dependency." → ✓ "She didn't miss him. She missed having someone to call at 2 AM."
**zh**: ✗ "那不是爱，是依赖" → ✓ "她不是想他，是想凌晨两点有个能打电话的人"

## P7: Minimize Lists of Three

### Xuanhuan

Cultivation descriptions love tricolons: "ancient, vast, and terrifying." Replace with a single vivid image.

**zh**: ✗ "古老、浩瀚、恐怖" → ✓ "像是坟墓里爬出来的气息"
**en**: ✗ "ancient, vast, and terrifying" → ✓ "It smelled like something that had been buried for centuries"

### Xianxia

Landscape descriptions: "mountains, rivers, and clouds." Replace with specific focal detail.

**zh**: ✗ "山峦、河流、云海" → ✓ "山尖上卡着一片云，怎么也飘不走"

### LitRPG

Item/skill descriptions may use lists by convention (e.g., "+10 STR, +5 DEX, +3 AGI"). This is acceptable in system UI. Narrative descriptions should still avoid tricolons.

### Modern

Emotional/sensory lists: "the noise, the heat, the press of bodies." Replace with one dominant detail.

**en**: ✗ "the noise, the heat, the press of bodies" → ✓ "Someone's elbow dug into her ribs, and the bass made her teeth itch"
**zh**: ✗ "噪音、热浪、人潮" → ✓ "有人的胳膊肘顶进她的肋骨，低音炮震得牙根发痒"

## Genre Prohibitions

- Coincidences that exist only to advance the plot without logical grounding
- Supporting characters becoming less intelligent specifically to make the MC look better
- Climaxes that arrive without earned buildup

## Narrative Guidance

Adjust narrative emphasis to match the specific story being told.

Maintain an unbroken chain of cause and effect.

Character behavior must be driven by motivation, not by what the plot requires at a given moment.
